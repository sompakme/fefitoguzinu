Step 2: Taming the Bureaucratic Beast (a.k.a. Requesting Your Pass Letter)

Now, let's wrangle that official documentation. Here's the lowdown:

  • Pay the fee (because adulting is expensive): There will likely be a processing fee. Consider it an investment in your glorious future as a licensed RN in New York!
