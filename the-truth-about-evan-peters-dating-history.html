The Truth About Evan Peters' Dating History

It seems that things started to go south for Evan Peters and Emma Roberts pretty early on in their relationship, since in 2013 the former couple was involved in a domestic violence incident while in Montreal. According to TMZ, Roberts was arrested after someone reported a fight in their hotel room and police found Peters with a bloody nose. Yet, Peters decided not to press charges and she was quickly released.

Not only did Peters not want his then-girlfriend in trouble, he was photographed just days later comforting her as she sobbed in his arms right after news of her arrest broke (per People). An unnamed source told Us Weekly at the time that, "Their romance is pretty extreme. They just behave in a way that's very passionate." A rep for both Peters and Roberts seemingly tried to downplay the arrest, saying (via People), "It was an unfortunate incident and misunderstanding," adding, "the couple are working together to move past it." 

While Roberts and Peters kept quiet about the whole thing, Roberts was quoted telling Nylon before the incident, "I can't say I'm never going to mess up, but if I do, I'll definitely be very sorry." She also later addressed the difficulties of having a relationship be so public to Cosmopolitan in 2019, saying,"It's hard enough to be with someone by yourself, let alone with an audience. Growing up is hard. Sometimes it makes me sad that I can't have a private moment."

If you or someone you know is dealing with domestic abuse, you can call the National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1−800−799−7233. You can also find more information, resources, and support at their website.
