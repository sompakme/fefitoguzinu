FAQs

Does HerRoom have free shipping?

HerRoom has free standard shipping on orders of over $75. If your order is less than this, there is a $7 standard shipping fee. Your order should arrive within 3-6 business days. You can also check this Marie Claire page for the occasional coupons for HerRoom free shipping. 

When are the HerRoom sales?

HerRoom has sales events throughout the year on its ‘Sale’ page. We’ve previously seen up to 70% off the original retail price on swimwear, bras, and sleepwear. Keep costs to an absolute minimum by shopping with a Herroom coupon. 

How do I get 10% off at HerRoom?

Sign up for HerRoom emails and you can get 10% off your first order. Visit their website and a sign-up message should appear. Simply type your full name and preferred email address before clicking ‘Redeem Now’.  

Can I track my HerRoom order?

Yes. You can track your order by going to the HerRoom website, scrolling down to ‘Customer Care’, and clicking ‘Track Order’. You will need to type your order number or zip code before clicking ‘See Your Order’. HerRoom is based in Texas and all standard shipping should arrive within 3-6 working days.  

What is the return policy at HerRoom?

HerRoom has free returns within 30 days of purchasing if you would like to. Your items should be returned like new. This means unworn, unwashed, odor-free, and with their original tags attached and packaging. Final sale items will not be accepted for return. HerRoom will make the refund to store credit or your HerRoom account.
