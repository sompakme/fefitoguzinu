Poseidon of Paros Hotel Review, Crete, Greece

Paros, Greece

Great service is a key element at this five-star seaside hotel surrounded by grass-spiked sand dunes near one of Paros’ best beaches. An elegant little spa offers treatments with organic Ariadne of Athens products and the main restaurant serves keenly priced international fare.
