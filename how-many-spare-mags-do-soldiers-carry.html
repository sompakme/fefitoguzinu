Do soldiers discard magazines?

From experience as an army reservist and from modern documentaries/books, soldiers are taught to retain their spent magazine on their kit after performing a reload. Usually if they are in a rush they would keep them in an easy access "dump bag" attached to their webbing/vest.

Do soldiers keep their weapons?
