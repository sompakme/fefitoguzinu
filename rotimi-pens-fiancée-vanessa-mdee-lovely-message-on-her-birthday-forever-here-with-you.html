Rotimi Pens Fiance Vanessa Mdee Lovely Message on Her Birthday: &quot;Forever Here with You&quot;

Nigerian-American singer and actor Rotimi has penned a romantic message to his fiancée Vanessa Mdee.

Rotimi showers Vanessa Mdee with love

Rotimi and Vanessa started dating in 2019, and in a previous interview, the mother of two said it was love at first sight.

The lovebirds revealed they first met at ESSENCE Festival and, surprisingly, Rotimi actually had another date lined up before he and Mdee crossed paths.

"I actually was there with somebody else, just a weekend friend, but she ended up getting sick. We were supposed to go to this party, and [she said] I don’t want to come. I said, 'okay, it's fine. I'm just going to go for 15 minutes.' I ended up going, and some of my castmates were also there. That's where I ended up meeting [Vanessa.] Fifteen minutes turned into an hour and a half conversation. Nothing else mattered in the room," Rotimi said in a previous interview with Essence.com.
