Magic Johnson's Daughter Elisa Johnson Escapes Home Invasion

Magic Johnson's daughter Elisa Johnson recently escaped a harrowing ordeal.  2 armed men staged an early morning home invasion at a San Fernando Valley Airbnb that Elisa and her friends were occupying for the weekend.

According to a report by TMZ, Johnson and her friends were resting in the rental home's various rooms when the men entered the home unwelcomed and began terrorizing the 10 guests, purportedly shoving them around the home and holding the unsuspecting members of the group at gunpoint. Law enforcement officials informed TMZ that it is unclear whether the suspects broke in, or if the main door was unlocked. 

23-year-old Elisa is said to have been in a bedroom at the time of the invasion and was allowed a moment of time to seek a solution upon hearing the commotion. TMZ informs that she fled through a sliding glass door at the back of the home, subsequently making her way ran down the street and out of harm's way. 

Ultimately, the invaders were able to leave the home before police arrived on the scene making off with jewelry, cash, a Rolex and electronics worth around 30-40 thousand dollars. Despite the unsettling incident, neither Elisa nor her friends were physically unharmed.
