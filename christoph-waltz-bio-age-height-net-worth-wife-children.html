The early life of Christoph Waltz

The talented actor was born in Vienna, Austria, on the 4th of October 1956. His father’s name was Johannes Waltz, and his mother is Elisabeth Urbancic, a famous Austrian costume designer.

Presently, Christoph Waltz age is 63 years, and his Zodiac sign is Libra. He has German roots from his father’s side.

After his birth, his father applied for him to become a German citizen. He, therefore, has dual citizenship in both Germany and Austria. He received Austrian citizenship in 2010.

Young Christoph Waltz was raised in a theatrical family. His maternal grandmother was Maria Mayen, an actress and Burgtheater, while Rudolf von Urban, his maternal grandfather, was a psychiatrist and Sigmund Freud’s student.

Both his step-grandfather and great-grandfather, Emmerich Reimers and Georg Reimers, respectively, were stage actors. Johannes Waltz, his biological father, passed away when he was only seven years old.

Educational background

The actor went to Theresianum in Vienna. After graduating, he studied acting at the Max Reinhardt Seminar. At the same time, he went to the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna to pursue singing and opera.

Eventually, he realized that his voice was not good enough for him to become an opera singer. He moved to New York City in the 1970s, where he studied method acting.
