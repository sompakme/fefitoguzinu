Lil Durk What Happened to Virgil Lyrics (Get the Full Lyrics)

 

Now, this track is like a poetic symphony by Lil Durk, featuring the incredible Gunna. It’s part of Lil Durk’s seventh studio album, “7220,” dropped in 202Chopsquad DJ sprinkled his magic as the producer, giving it that extra oomph.

The lyrics echo Lil Durk’s deep admiration for Virgil Abloh, the influential fashion designer. The collaboration with Gunna adds a dynamic layer, making it a profound tribute to a creative genius.

Breaking Down the Lyrics – In Simple Words

Alright, let’s break down some of the lyrics. Lil Durk starts by reminiscing about the times Virgil said he was proud. But, sadly, there were doubters, and life took a tragic turn. Lil Durk weaves his strategy for success magically, expressing his resilience against those questioning his journey.

He talks about his early struggles, visiting the doctor before hitting 12, and even his adventures with a certain Roxy. Lil Durk emphasizes his royalty, proclaiming he’s a king and his crew is good.

Fast forward, he touches on serious topics, from survival lessons with his TT to dealing with disloyalty. The lyrics emphasize the importance of loyalty and avoiding unnecessary drama.

The Virgil Abloh Connection – A Creative Genius Remembered

Now, let’s talk about Virgil Abloh. Lil Durk expresses his love for the late designer and wonders about the events leading to Virgil’s passing. It’s an emotional journey as Lil Durk wishes his brother had made it through surgery. These lines hit deep, reflecting on the pain and hurt he feels.

Gunna joins in, adding his thoughts on Virgil’s influence and the impact of his passing. The lyrics bring together themes of loss, reflections on life, and a tribute to a legend.

Understanding the Meaning of “What Happened to Virgil”

The song holds a profound meaning, acting as a tribute to Virgil Abloh. Virgil, known for his groundbreaking work with Off-White and Louis Vuitton, passed away after battling cancer. Lil Durk and Gunna use the song to express their grief, questioning the circumstances around Virgil’s departure.

The lyrics touch on personal struggles, the impact of Virgil’s legacy, and the void left in their lives. Lil Durk beautifully captures the essence of Virgil’s influence on the creative world.

About Lil Durk – The Man Behind the Lyrics

Now, let’s talk about Lil Durk, the creative force behind this powerful song. His real name is Durk Derrick Banks, and he’s an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Born in Chicago, he’s the lead member of the collective Only the Family (OTF).

Lil Durk’s journey to fame includes impressive mixtapes like “Signed to the Streets.” His unique style caught the attention of Def Jam Recordings, leading to a record deal. While his debut albums like “Remember My Name” and “Lil Durk 2X” showcased his skills, Lil Durk eventually parted ways with Def Jam in 2018, marking a new chapter. His authenticity, raw storytelling, and versatile flow have made Lil Durk a notable figure in the rap scene. He continues to captivate audiences worldwide with impactful music and compelling storytelling.

In Conclusion – A Musical Homage to Virgil

Wrapping it up, “What Happened to Virgil” is more than just a song; it’s a heartfelt tribute to Virgil Abloh. Lil Durk’s lyrics, accompanied by Gunna’s verses, create a musical narrative that pays homage to a creative genius. The emotions, struggles, and reflections embedded in the song make it a timeless piece in Lil Durk’s discography. Let’s keep Virgil’s legacy alive through the powerful medium of music. Until next time, stay tuned for more musical explorations!
