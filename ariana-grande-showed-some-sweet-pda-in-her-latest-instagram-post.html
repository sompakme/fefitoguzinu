Ariana Grande Showed Some Sweet PDA in Her Latest Instagram Post

A kiss on the cheek may be quite continental, but Ariana Grande's husband, Dalton Gomez, chose to give her a peck on the lips, instead. Dressed to the nines, the couple shared a sweet kiss in Grande's latest Instagram post. In the shot, Grande has on a lacy LBD with a high slit and towering stiletto heels — her signature ponytail was there, no worries — while Gomez wore a grey suit. The carousel includes a photo of the two hugging before a swipe revealed the kiss in a short video.

She captioned the post with a heart-and-arrow emoji.

Grande and Gomez have been married since May 2021. The two opted for a very intimate ceremony in Montecito, California, and broke the news on Instagram before sharing photos of the big day with Vogue. Though she's kept this relationship out of the headlines after high-profile flings with Pete Davidson and Big Sean, sources close to the couple say that they're loving married life.

"Ariana and Dalton have really been enjoying married life," a friend told Entertainment Tonight. "Ariana is happy and feels very relaxed. She feels like she is entering a new chapter of life and is excited to see where it goes."

"Ariana and Dalton felt like before they were married, they couldn't be as open about their relationship without it being scrutinized in the media and now they feel way more at ease," the source added. "They have plans to travel more together and feel closer than ever."
