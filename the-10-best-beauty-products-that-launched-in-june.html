The 10 Best Beauty Products That Launched in June

June is exciting for many reasons (summer solstice anyone? Hoping everyone is booking their summer adventures — whether abroad or local — ASAP). But leave it to our favorite beauty brands to up the ante and give us all something to talk about it.

We've got Guerlain and its bigger foray into haircare, Sofia Vergara launching a suncare collection, and some of the best complexion and body products to get you that summer glow. Below, check out the 10 best beauty launches this month had to offer.

Guerlain Revitalising & Fortifying Shampoo

To shop: $82; guerlain.com

Guerlain's expansion into the hair care category was well worth the wait. The Abeille Royal Scalp & Hair Care Collection — which contains the Revitalising & Fortifying Shampoo, a Repairing and Replumping Care Conditioner, a Double R Radiance & Repair Mask, and Hair Brush — uses black bee honey to nourish and treat any damage to your strands. The shampoo in particular has caffeine to strengthen the scalp and goes on lightweight to give your hair a refreshing cleanse while leaving it super soft.

Avène Cleanance Acne Medicated Clearing Treatment

To shop: $32; avene.com

Everyone's favorite thermal water and French pharmacy brand has launched something that will make fellow acne-prone peeps rejoice with glee. The Cleanance Collection is Avene's first medicated acne-clearing products aimed at helping you get the clearest skin possible. The collection consists of two products: the Acne Medicated Clearing Gel Cleanser and the Clearing Treatment, both use salicylic acid, micronized Benzoyl Peroixde, and the Avène thermal spring water to treat those pesky breakouts, soothe irritation, and reinforce the skin barrier.

Toty Solaria Mineral SPF 50+

To Shop: $42; toty.com

Sofia Vergara has entered the celebrity beauty game with her own collection. In collaboration with Cantabria Labs, the actress launched Toty, a skin care, makeup, wellness, and sun care line. The products use an antioxidant-rich in bioactive compounds to protect skin from sun damage and from environmental aggressors.

Pattern & Sergio Hudson Curl-Conscious Robe

To Shop: $88; patternbeauty.com

There's really nothing quite like a good robe, and Pattern might have just the one you need this summer. In collaboration with fashion house Sergio Hudson, the beauty brand launched the Curl-Conscious Robe. It's a highly absorbent, soft, and lightweight robe that is made with sustainable fabric in Hudson's signature silhouette of strong defined shoulders and a cinched waist. But the best part is that it has a low neckline and short sleeves that won't disrupt your curls from drying or getting in the way of any hair styling. Available in sizes XS to XXL for anyone to lounge in, you'll want to get your hands on this one quick.

YSE Beauty Liquid Glow Body Oil

To Shop: $58; ysebeauty.com

Summer always calls for a dewy, shimmery body oil, and this latest one has found a permanent spot in our rotation. The YSE Beauty body oil gives you a slight shimmer for that vacation-y glow without overwhelming you with glitter. It is also deeply nourishing thanks to ingredients like vitamin E and meadow foam seed and grape seed oil.

Westman Atelier Liquid Super Loaded

To Shop: $58; westman-atelier.com

Speaking of getting your skin to glow, if you want to up your complexion game, then this is the product for you. Westman Atelier's Liquid Super Loaded is packed with nourishing skin ingredients like vitamin C and avocado seed and Tsubaki oil to even out skin tone and hydrate skin. It also contains pearls that reflect light for natural-looking radiance and dewy finish.

Bluestone Sunshields x PillowtalkDerm Major Shade Full Lux Sunshield

To Shop: $70; pillowtalkderm.com

The talk of a visor while lounging by the pool or on your favorite beach may not sound appealing, but you'll want to hear us out with this launch. The Bluestone Sunshields x PillowtalkDerm Major Shade Full Lux Sunshield is a heat-resistant sun visor that blocks against 99% of UVA/UVB rays. But because we're all about pretty aesthetics, it comes with an adjustable lavender lens that just looks super cool. Function and style all in one.

Colourpop Ignite Lite Stix

To Shop: $10; colourpop.com

If you loved the Blush Stix, then this is right up your alley. Colourpop launched its sister bronzing product to give you that warm "I-just-got-back-from-a-fabulous-beach-vacay" glow. It's a lightweight creamy texture that blends in so seamlessly, and you can pretty much take it with you wherever you go.

Mad Rabbit Tattoo Repair Patch

To Shop: $25; madrabbit.com

Tattoo care just got a whole lot better thanks to this innovative healing patch. It contains ingredients like D-Panthenol, olive leaf extract, and vitamin E that will attract water molecules to form a protective layer over your new ink. All those nourishing ingredients help reduce inflammation, soothe irritation, repair damage, and protect the skin during the healing process. You'll definitely want to use this for your next (or first) tattoo.

Fresh x The Summer I Turned Pretty Limited-Edition Lip Set

To Shop: $34.50; fresh.com

To celebrate the premiere of Amazon Prime's hit show The Summer I Turned Pretty, Fresh debuted a limited-edition set of the beloved Sugar Lip Treatment. You get three different shades — Sugar Radiant Rose, Sugar Dewy Daisy, and Sugar Lily Luster — ranging in pinks and nudes for the ideal summer lip. So whether you're Team Conrad or Team Jeremiah, this lip kit is for everyone.
