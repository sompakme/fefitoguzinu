Mena Massoud (born 17 September 1991) is a creative Egyptian-Canadian actor and singer.

He also appeared as Jared Malik in the 2015 Canadian drama series Open Heart, Ethan Hart in the Hulu series titled Reprisal, and Tarek Kassar in the Amazon Prime series titled Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan 2018.
