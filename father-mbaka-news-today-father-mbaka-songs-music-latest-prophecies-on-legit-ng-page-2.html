  • Name: Father Ejike Mbaka
  • Birthdate: July 28, 1957
  • Birthplace: Enugu, Nigeria
  • Occupation: Catholic priest and a musician

Early Life and Background

  • Father Mbaka was born into the late Chief Humphrey Ogbuefi family and Felicia Mbaka of Amata Ituku in Awgu L.G.A of Enugu State.
  • His father’s family was known for their talent in palm wine tapping in the Ituku community.

Priesthood

  • Father Mbaka attended Seat of Wisdom Seminary in Owerri, Imo State and St Joseph’s Seminary in Ikot Ekpene, Akwa Ibom State, where he was trained for the priesthood.
  • He was ordained a priest of the Roman Catholic Church on July 29, 1995.

Personal Life

As a Catholic priest, Father Mbaka has taken a vow of celibacy and is not married.

Videos

Father Mbaka has released several videos of his preaching and music on his YouTube channel: Rev. Fr. Ejike Mbaka.
