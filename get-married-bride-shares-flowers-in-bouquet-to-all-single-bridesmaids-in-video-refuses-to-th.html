A thoughtful bride earned the admiration of netizens as she shared each flower in her bouquet with all her single bridesmaids.

A video that went viral on TikTok showed the moment the bride faced her bridesmaids as she distributed parts of the bouquet instead of throwing it into the air for singles to catch.

Each bridesmaid appeared emotional and smiled as they received flowers from the elegantly dressed bride.

The clip had amassed over 323k views when making this report. Many people commended the bride for going against the norm and having the single bridesmaids in mind.
