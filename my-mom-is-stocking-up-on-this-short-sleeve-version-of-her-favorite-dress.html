My Mom Is Stocking Up on This Short-Sleeve Version of Her Favorite Dress

Spring is here, and my mom and I are so happy to be able to pull out our favorite maxi dresses from last summer; for me, that’s my AnotherChill maxi and for my mom, it’s this Anrabess dress she now owns in four colors. In fact, she tried it on recently and said while she loves it, she wishes it had sleeves for a bit more coverage on those cooler days. So, we took to Amazon to find a similar style, and to our surprise, the brand behind her go-to maxi dress, Anrabess, also sells a short-sleeve version — and it’s currently on sale with an on-site coupon.

The short-sleeve maxi comes in 24 color and print options including black, green, pink, purple, blue, tie-dye, floral, and speckled, and in sizes S through XXL. It features a scooped neckline, two side slits that fall around the knee depending on your height, and, the best part, side seam pockets. It’s made from a stretchy knit fabric that’s easy to pull on, comfy, and flattering. 

Anrabess Short-Sleeve Maxi Dress 

The wide selection of colors and prints with more than 5,300 five-star ratings made this T-shirt dress an easy add to cart for my mom. If it’s anything like the tank version she already owns, she said she’ll be back for more — and based on the rave reviews from Amazon shoppers, it’s safe to say she will be. One reviewer who bought the dress over two years ago wrote they’ve gone back and bought two more colors because it’s “great quality” and “comfy.” Another shopper called it “flattering” and said it’s easy to “dress it up or down,” adding they “could wear [it] for hours.” 

One reviewer said they “get compliments every time [they] wear the dress,” noting it’s “snug in all the right places.” Another wrote they made the dress their work-from-home “uniform,” because they wear it so much, comparing its feel to that of comfy pajamas. As a cherry on top, they mention it “washes like a dream.” In other words, feel free to wear it all the time, because it’s easy to launder and even easier to wear. 

Shop the Anrabess Short-Sleeve Maxi Dress along with the tank version while both are on sale now. Like my mom, you’ll probably be living in them for the seasons to come.
