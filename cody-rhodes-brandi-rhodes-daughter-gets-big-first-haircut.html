Cody Rhodes &amp; Brandi Rhodes Daughter Gets Big First Haircut

Cody Rhodes and his wife, Brandi Rhodes, are not only popular names in the wrestling business, but one of the most family-oriented people as well. Both are spending lot of time with each other and their little daughter as Cody recovers from his injury. Recently, their daughter was in for her first-ever bangs trim.

Cody and Brandi Rhodes met during the latter first run in WWE and got married to each other in 2013. They eventually helped found AEW, but they eventually left as Cody returned to WWE. Meanwhile, the couple welcomed their first child together in June 2021, a daughter they named Liberty Iris Runnels.

The youngest member of the Rhodes family recently got her first-ever trim done. Brandi Rhodes posted a video on her Instagram story where her daughter is seen getting her first bangs trimmed.

“First bangs trim.”

We can hope to see Cody and Brandi Rhodes bring their daughter on the road sometime after the American Nightmare returns to WWE television programming from his torn pectoral muscle injury. Only time will tell how much television time is in Liberty Iris Rhodes’ future.
