Kihei Fourth Friday Town Party scheduled for Friday | News, Sports, Jobs

The Kihei Fourth Friday Town Party will be held from 6 to 9 p.m. Friday at the Azeka Shopping Center Mauka.

The monthly event features live music, food booths and trucks, arts, crafts and shopping with local retailers.

Free entertainment will be provided at the main stage by Arlie-Avery Asiu starting at 6:10 p.m., Dance Maui at 7:25 p.m. and James Somera, Lenny Castellanos, Kenny Gieser and Benny Uyetake at 7:45 p.m.

Missy Aguilar will perform at 6:15 p.m. at the food court.

Maui Classic Cruisers will also be on display near the Keiki Zone.

Free parking for the event is located at Azeka Makai.

For more information, visit kiheifridays.com or facebook.com/kiheifridays.
