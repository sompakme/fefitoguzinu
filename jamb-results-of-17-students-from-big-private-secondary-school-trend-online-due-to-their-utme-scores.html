JAMB Results of 17 Students From Big Private Secondary School Trend Online Due to Their UTME Scores

  • A big private school in the southeast is being praised online due to the performance of its students in the JAMB examination
  • The UTME results of 17 students seen online showed that the candidates performed well, with many of them scoring above 300 marks
  • Eight students had above 200, while nine students got above 300 marks out of an aggregate score of 400

The JAMB exam results of some students from a school in the southeast are trending online.

The Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) result was released on Monday, April 29, and candidates have been checking theirs.

The 17 candidates are from Shekinah British International School, Ihiagwa, described as one of the biggest schools in the southeast.

The results were shared by Mpaa Collins, who praised the students for their outstanding performance.

According to the results shared on X, eight students scored above 200, while nine students scored above 300 marks out of the 400 obtainable in the JAMB UTME.

Anyiam David was the school's highest-scoring JAMB candidate, scoring 339 marks, followed by Opara David, who scored 333.

Buruku Belina was another student who scored above 300. She had 317 and was followed by Merenu Styles, who scored 315.

The result also showed that Anyiam Chukwuma scored 314, closely followed by Eze Precious, who had 311.
