Is Mordekaiser AP or AD?

Mordekaiser is an AP champion in League of Legends. He does magic damage with his passive, his Q, and his E ability. Morde's W and R are utility spells and don't deal any damage. Mordekaiser scales with Ability Power, so the more AP he has, the harder his abilities hit.
