Bruce Willis: how many kids does he have? Inside the actor's blended family amid health battle

Meet Bruce's girls amid health battle

    Bruce Willis, the renowned Hollywood actor, is known for his incredibly close-knit and supportive family. 

    Following the recent announcement of his dementia diagnosis, his family has shown immense love and support, including his former wife Demi Moore. 

    The actor's large family often appears together in social media posts, but how many children does Bruce actually have?

    Rumer Glenn Willis 

    Born on August 16, 1988, in Paducah, Kentucky, Rumer is the eldest daughter of Bruce Willis and Demi Moore. Raised away from Hollywood in Hailey, Idaho, Rumer made her onscreen debut at the age of five in her mother's film . 

    In recent years, she has continued acting in films and TV shows and even appeared in several reality shows like and . Rumer announced in December 2022 that she is expecting her first child with her boyfriend Derek Richard Thomas.

    Scout LaRue Willis 

    Born on July 20, 1991, in Sun Valley, Idaho, Scout is the couple's second daughter. After graduating in 2013, Scout became an artist and activist for body politics. Recently, she has focused on music, releasing her debut single in 2021, followed by her self-titled album a year later.

    Tallulah Belle Willis 

    The youngest child of Bruce and Demi, Tallulah Belle, was born on February 3, 1994, in Los Angeles. After a successful stay in rehab to address her eating disorders, Tallulah managed to mend her strained relationship with her family. In 2021, she announced her engagement to director Dillon Buss and has since launched her own clothing brand, Wyllis.

    Mabel Ray Willis 

    Bruce and his second wife, Emma Heming, welcomed their first child together, Mabel Ray, on April 1, 2012. Raised in Bedford Hills, New York, Mabel is currently in elementary school and aspires to become a 'shark' on the hit TV show one day.

    Evelyn Penn Willis

    Emma and Bruce's second daughter, Evelyn Penn, was born on May 5, 2014. On Emma's Instagram, Evelyn has shown her love for Star Wars, rollercoasters, and various other activities.

    With five loving children, Bruce Willis' family has come together in solidarity during this challenging time. The actor's blended family is a testament to the strong bonds they share and the unwavering support they offer one another. 

    From acting and music to activism and fashion, each of Bruce's children has pursued their unique passions, carving out their own paths in life while remaining united as a family.
