Twilight star Gregory Tyree Boyce dies aged 30 alongside girlfriend Natalie Adepoju

- Twilight movie star Gregory Boyce and his girlfriend have passed away

- The two were reportedly found dead in their Las Vegas apartment

- Boyce left behind a 10-year-old daughter, mother and other family members

It is indeed a trying period for the family members of Hollywood actor Gregory Tyree Boyce who is mostly known for his role as Tyler Crowley in Twilight.

According to reports by Mirror, the movie star aged 30 and his girlfriend Natalie Adepoju were found dead.

The report noted that the Las Vegas Medical Examiner’s office confirmed the passing of the two lovers on May 13, with the cause of death still unknown.

It was reported that an undisclosed source claimed that the actor’s cousin woke up in their house and discovered that his car was still parked out front.
