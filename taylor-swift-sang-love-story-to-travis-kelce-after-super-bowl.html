Taylor Swift Sang &quot;Love Story&quot; to Travis Kelce After Super Bowl

It was happily ever after for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce following the Kansas City Chiefs' Super Bowl win on Sunday night. After exiting Vegas's Allegiant Stadium, the couple moved their PDA from the field to the dance floor and celebrated the championship moment in the most meta-slash-fairytale way possible. 

In fan footage captured at the Chiefs after-party, Swift and Kelce can be seen dancing while a techno version of her song "Love Story" plays in the background. The pop star, who was wearing the tight end's sparkly black jacket from earlier, sings the lyrics, "It's a love story, baby, just say, 'Yes'" to him before they kiss. 

Another video showed them holding hands while leaving the club together in the wee hours of the morning:

Last night, the Super Bowl went into overtime with the Chiefs ultimately defeating the San Francisco 49ers 25-22. Throughout the game, Taylor cheered on Travis from her private suite with Blake Lively, Ice Spice, and the entire Kelce clan, and once the Chiefs scored the winning final points, the singer was spotted jumping up and down in excitement and lifting up Lively.

Taylor — wearing a black corset with matching rhinestone cutout jeans and a $3,000 diamond necklace that not-so-subtly nodded to her relationship — made her way onto the field to congratulate Travis with a hug and several kisses.

What else can we say? It's a love story.
