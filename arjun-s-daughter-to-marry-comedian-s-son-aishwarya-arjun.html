Arjun's Daughter To Marry Comedian's Son Aishwarya Arjun

Popular Kannada actor Arjun Sarja is gearing up for a grand release with the highly anticipated film “Leo,” starring Thalapathy Vijay and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. Meanwhile, the rumors are swirling that his daughter, Aishwarya Arjun, is preparing to embark on a new journey by getting married.

If the reports are believed to be true, Aishwarya Arjun is reportedly set to tie the knot with Umapathy Ramaiah, an actor and the son of National Award-winning actor-comedian Thambi Ramaiah.

The Sources indicate that Aishwarya Arjun and Umapathy Ramaiah have been in a committed relationship for quite some time, and now they have decided to take their bond to the next level.

While an official announcement regarding the wedding is yet to be made, it is anticipated that the family will make it official soon.

Aishwarya Arjun was supposed to make her debut in the Telugu film industry as a heroine with Vishwaksen’s film, directed by Arjun. But the project has been kept on hold after Vishwak walked out of the film.

Aishwarya, on the other hand, made her debut in Tamil and Kannada cinema.
