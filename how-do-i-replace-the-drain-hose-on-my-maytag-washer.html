Is it possible to expand the length of a washing machine drain hose?

It is not recommended to lengthen the length of a drain hose. It is necessary to bring the drainage system closer to the appliance, for example, by extending the piping to the washer. The drain pump is intended to pump water just as far as the drain hose will allow it to go, and no farther.
