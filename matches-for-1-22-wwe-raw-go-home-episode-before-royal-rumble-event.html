Matches For 1/22 WWE RAW Go-Home Episode Before Royal Rumble Event

The 2024 Royal Rumble is going down on January 27th. That show should be a massive show, and next week’s RAW will start that road.

Ringside News had complete coverage of WWE RAW’s January 15th episode. They included a lot in that show, including setting up a few things for next week’s RAW.

CM Punk and Cody Rhodes are set to have a face-to-face next week on RAW. This is in response to the challenge that Punk laid down during RAW this week. Also, Ivy Nile will take on Valhalla next week on RAW.

Ivar will be in action as well, as he takes on Chad Gable. Also, Drew McIntyre and Damian Priest will throw down on RAW.

We will have to see how the January 22nd episode of WWE RAW stacks up when everything is said and done. Obviously, the Royal Rumble can prepare fans for a massive show coming up in Tropicana Field.

WWE RAW 1/22 Card:

  • Cody Rhodes & CM Punk face-to-face
  • Damian Priest vs Drew McIntyre
  • Ivy Nile vs Valhalla
  • Chad Gable vs Ivar
