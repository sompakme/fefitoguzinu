You Should Lead the Guild: Ernest Obi Leads Procession for Jnr Pope, Shares How Much Producers

  • Ernest Obi has joined his other colleagues in mourning late Jnr Pope as he leads procession for the actor
  • Junior Pope and some crew members lost their lives in a boat mishap a few days ago while going for a movie shot in the Riverine area
  • Obi also shared how producers maltreat some crew members and advised those experiencing such to report to their guild

Nollywood actor, Ernest Obi, has mourned his Jnr Pope by leading a procession for him and calling for a change in the movie industry.

Legit.ng had reported that Jnr Pope died while on his way to the movie production in the riverine area. Some crew members also lost their lives in the boat mishap.

In the video sighted online, the actor addressed people who joined the possession and he lamented bitterly of how producers treat their crew members. He said some people were paid N10k.

Obi calls for change

In the viral video, Ernest Obi recounted how crew members endure working in the rain and sun for minimal pay, while actors relax indoors, receiving hefty compensation.

The top movie star noted that crew members should report to their guild if they don't get treated well.

He also said if the crew members had made that call, the boat mishap wouldn't have happened because the guild would have acted before such an incident and not after it.
