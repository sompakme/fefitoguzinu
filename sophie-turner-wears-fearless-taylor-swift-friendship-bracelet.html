Sophie Turner Wears &quot;Fearless&quot; Taylor Swift Friendship Bracelet

Sophie Turner is learning to drop Easter eggs from the queen of clues herself, Taylor Swift.

The Game of Thrones star made her return to Instagram over the weekend by posting a photo on her Story of her friendship bracelet from new pal Swift, who made the DIY jewelry popular during her Eras Tour. Turner’s was a soft-pink beaded bracelet with white beads in the middle that read “Fearless.”

While “Fearless” is the title of one of Swift’s hit singles and her second album, the accessory seems to have a double meaning, as Turner is going through a difficult divorce from her husband of four years, Joe Jonas. The Jonas Brothers singer and guitarist filed for divorce from Turner on September 5—something which she alleges she learned from the media, rather than from him—and since then, the two have been hashing out the details of their separation and custody agreement, as they share two young daughters, Willa and Delphine.

courtesy of Sophie Turner via Instagram

As she deals with the divorce proceedings, Turner has been living in Swift’s New York City apartment with her kids. The actor has been seen leaving the TriBeCa home various times over the past couple weeks, and has also been spending plenty of time with Swift outside of the apartment. The friends cheered on Travis Kelce at his New Jersey game last week with Swift’s celebrity posse (including Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds), and have gone out on girls’ dinners on several occasions.

Jonas, meanwhile, is currently on tour in the U.S. with his brothers.
