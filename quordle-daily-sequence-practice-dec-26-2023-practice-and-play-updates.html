What is Quordle?

For those unfamiliar with Quordle, it’s a game that challenges you to guess five-letter words. It shares similarities with Wordle, but with a twist – each guess contributes letters to four words simultaneously. Picture yourself juggling four Wardle games at once. Sounds a bit daunting, right? Don’t worry; it’s way more fun than it sounds!

Now, let’s talk rules. Every game has them, right? In Quordle, there are five rules to keep in mind:

  • Letters in the answer and in the right place turn green.
  • Letters in the answer but in the wrong place turn yellow.
  • Letters not in the answer turn gray.
  • Answers are never plural.
  • You must complete the daily Quordle before midnight.
