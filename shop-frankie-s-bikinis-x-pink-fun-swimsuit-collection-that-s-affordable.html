Shop Frankies Bikinis X PINK Fun Swimsuit Collection Thats Affordable

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, SheKnows may receive an affiliate commission.

With the current weather, it doesn’t feel like spring is on the horizon, but the warmer months will arrive before you know it. Naturally, that can only mean that Spring Break is right around the corner. Regardless of what you and your family will be doing this year, one thing is certain: new swimwear is a must for everyone. And believe it or not, this is the best time to shop. While you could wait for summer to arrive, by then, all the best styles will be long gone. Take it from me; as someone who grew up in the Sunshine State, I can tell you a thing or two about quality swimsuits but, most importantly, when to get them — which is now. Luckily for us, Frankies Bikinis just partnered up with PINK for a sweet collection that’s actually very affordable.

In case you’re unfamiliar, Frankies Bikinis is a chic swim and loungewear brand that’s done collabs with a wide range of celebs from Sydney Sweeney to Pamala Anderson — and now they’ve teamed up with none other than Victoria’s Secret’s PINK to bring you and the teen in your life new swimsuits.

Typically, Frankie’s Bikinis is on the higher end of swimwear when it comes to price. A simple two-piece bikini can cost you upwards of $150, while one-pieces start at $180 — clearly, that’s not cheap by any means. But with PINK by Frankie’s Bikinis, you can get similar swim styles that start at $35 with adorable cover-up options to match for just $25.

In my opinion, both brands make top-tier swimwear that are of excellent quality — I remember spending plenty of spring breaks decked out in VS Pink — so it only makes sense that the two would come together for a phenomenal collab. The new collection features fun and flirty silhouettes adorned in timeless colors like pink and black. There’s also a yummy strawberry and cherry print that’s very on point with classic Frankies Bikinis designs. 

Below, shop a handful of our favorite new styles that we can’t wait to add to our collections. But hurry — sizes have already begun to sell out!

Augusta Bikini Top + Golden Hour Bikini Bottom

The red border against the classic pink hue makes this halter-neck bikini a timeless swimsuit for years to come. This is a great option for someone looking for a bit more support while still being on trend.

Hudson Bikini Top + Bottom

Everything about this ruffle-edge bikini is downright adorable. The off-the-shoulder top is a bit more unique than your typical halter, triangle, or strapless styles, and the little strawberry and cherries printed throughout just scream summer. And if you like the style, but not crazy about the print — don’t worry; it’s also available in black too.

Marlow One-Piece Swimsuit

Everyone needs a fun one-piece, and this berry-printed one is it. It has a bold red border with a low-back silhouette that’s both cute and flirty while still having support.

Cape May Bikini Top + Rosemary Bikini Bottom

Like an LBD, everyone should have a little black bikini in their swimwear collection. This triangle top and string bottom feature a tiny 3D red rosette in the center, adding something that takes it beyond a plain black swimsuit.

Brooklyn Cotton Tee

And this berry-adorned crewneck t-shirt is the perfect cover-up to finish the look.
