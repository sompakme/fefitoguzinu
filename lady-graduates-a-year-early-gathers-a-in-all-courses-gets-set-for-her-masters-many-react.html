A lady identified as Tuana Yazici has celebrated her achievement after graduating with distinction from the higher institution.

Tuana said she took 27 credits in her last semester in school to graduate early and made A+ in all her courses.

Tuana said she will be doing her masters in International Administration before going to law school.

She wrote on LinkedIn:

"I don’t usually post grades but I took 27 credits this semester to graduate a year early. It was tremendously hard to keep up with classes and research jobs outside of school, especially during this time. Beyond honored that they all came back as A+’s! Always got to finish strong!
