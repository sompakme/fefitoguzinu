Why is 'CoryxKenshin dead' trending?

CORYXKENSHIN is an American YouTuber best known for his light hearted and comedic playthroughs of horror games.

A rumor circulating has suggested that the streamer has passed away. Here, we explain why this has been said.

Who is CoryxKenshin?

Coryxkenshin is an American YouTuber.

He was born on in November 9, 1992.

His real name is Cory DeVante Williams.

He joined YouTube in 2009 and has since amassed over 15 million subscribers on the site.

He is best known for his comedic play through of horror games.

In 2021, he ranked as the fourth top creator in the US based on subscriber growth.

He has a brother called Anthony and a dog named Samson the Savage.

Despite being unexplained, he has took multiple hiatuses during his time as a content creator.
