Taylor Swift Is in the House for Super Bowl LVIII

Football’s biggest night of the year is here: Super Bowl LVIII. Fans across the nation and beyond can expect stellar plays from the Kansas City Chiefs and San Francisco 49ers, an electric halftime performance by Usher, Reba McEntire, Post Malone, Andra Day, and Tiësto, and of course—to make things even grander—Taylor Swift.

That’s right: The 14-time Grammy winner is in the house for the football championship. Anyone worried about the “Karma” songstress not making it to Las Vegas in time, after performing in Tokyo, need not fret anymore, as she has been seen arriving at Allegiant Stadium—along with bestie Blake Lively and rapper Ice Spice—ready to cheer on her man, Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce. According to the Associated Press, Swift left Tokyo on Saturday night following her four Eras Tour shows in the Japanese capital, and flew to Los Angeles, arriving Saturday afternoon due to the time difference. She traveled from L.A. to Las Vegas.

Ezra Shaw//Getty Images

Ezra Shaw//Getty Images

With each passing week of the most recent NFL season, Swift’s game-day fits have only gotten better—and for the most important game of them all, she pulled out all the stops. The music mogul brought some bling today, sporting black straight-leg jeans by Area with a crystal-adorned slit on each thigh, which she paired with a sultry, slightly sheer black crochet corset top from Dion Lee. For a pop of Kansas City red, she carried a vibrant varsity jacket that featured white stripe details along the cuffs, hem, and shoulders and was emblazoned with the number 60 (the year the team was founded) in black stitching. Swift accented the look with gold bracelets, dainty gold earrings, a glittering chain necklace that featured a small red crystal pendant, and a gold chain necklace with her beau’s jersey number, 87. She swept her luscious locks up into a high ponytail and glammed up with her trademark red lip and black winged eyeliner.

Gossip Girl alum Lively went all out in red, donning a bold Adidas tracksuit over a simple cropped white tank top, along with sizzling red pointed-toe pumps and a matching clutch bag. Lively finished off her look with a chunky gold necklace and matching bracelet, and styled her hair in voluminous waves. “Deli” rapper Ice Spice, on the other hand, also opted for black, wearing a figure-hugging, long-sleeved maxi dress, matching leather boots, and a coordinated purse.

The Super Bowl marks the 13th game Swift has attended this season. That, of course, is the singer’s magic number, and Swifties were quick to find other instances of it in the game’s details.
