The Creed Brothers Make Their WWE Main Roster Debut On 10/30 RAW

WWE has a roster full of Superstars in NXT who all want to make their mark on the main roster. That opportunity finally came for Julius and Brutus Creed this week on RAW, and Ivy Nile was right with them.

Ringside News provided complete play-by-play coverage this week. It was quite a show, even thought they were forced to book a special episode due to so many members of the roster being out of the country, or otherwise busy, due to their Saudi Arabia trip.

The Creed Brothers accepted the Alpha Academy’s open challenge to face them on RAW this week. Michael Cole noted on commentary that the Creed Brothers were “coming up” from NXT.

The Creed Brothers and Ivy Nile made their way to the ring, and they got things started on RAW against the Alpha Academy. Their match lasted two segments, and just before the commercial, Michael Cole welcomed the Creed Brothers to WWE.

Sean Sapp noted behind Fightful’s paywall that, “We have not yet been told that the Creed Brothers are an official call-up.” That being said, they made quite an impression during their first outing on the red brand.

In the end, the Creed Brothers won the match, and this was a very impressive showing for them. Wade Barrett made a comment about how RAW and SmackDown are probably drafting contracts for the Creed Brothers right now.

Keep checking back with us here at Ringside News for more. You never know what can happen next, but it seems that the Creed Brothers are going to start making their place on the main roster.
