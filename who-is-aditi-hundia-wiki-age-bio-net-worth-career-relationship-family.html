Who is Aditi Hundia? | Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

Spread the love             
  • Aditi Hundia is a well-known Indian model, Instagram influencer, and former beauty pageant titleholder from Jaipur, Rajasthan.
  • She gained recognition after participating in the Femina Miss India 2017 pageant.
  • Aditi achieved the title of Miss India Supranational 2018 in Miss Diva 2018, allowing her to represent India in the Miss Universe 2018 pageant.

Early Life, Family & Education:

  • Aditi Hundia was born on January 15, 1997, in Jaipur, Rajasthan, making her 23 years old as of 2020.
  • She shares a close bond with her father Lalit Hundia, who has always encouraged her, and her beautiful mother Babita Hundia.
  • Aditi has a brother named Yash Hundia, who is a Mechanical Engineer and IndiGo Cadet Pilot.
  • She completed her secondary school education at the Indian International School and holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration from St. Xavier’s College.

Physical Statistics & More:

  • Aditi Hundia has a stunning appearance with a height of approximately 5 feet 6 inches (168 cm) and weighs around 53 kilograms (117 lbs).
  • She maintains an hourglass figure with body measurements of 36-24-36 and wears a bra size of 34D.
  • Aditi has captivating hazel eyes and dark brown hair, which she often experiments with by dyeing it in lighter shades.
  • Her fashion choices include bodycon dresses, tops, and elegant sarees during festival seasons.

Personal Life, Boyfriend & Relationships:

  • Aditi Hundia has been in a romantic relationship with Indian cricketer Ishan Kishan since 2018.
  • Ishan Kishan represents the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League and has played for the Jharkhand Cricket Team.
  • Aditi has been a supportive partner, attending Ishan’s matches and cheering him on during the IPL finals.

Career & Net Worth:

  • Aditi Hundia pursued her modeling career in 2016 after participating in the Elite Miss Rajasthan contest, where she became the first runner-up.
  • She gained prominence by winning the title of Femina Miss India Rajasthan 2017.
  • Aditi has collaborated with various brands, appeared in music videos, and worked with renowned fashion designers.
  • Her estimated net worth is around Rs 35 lakhs Indian Rupees as of 2020.

Trivia:

  • Aditi Hundia actively supports charitable organizations such as Rise Against Hunger and Fight Against Cancer.
  • She initiated a campaign through the NGO Ketto to help children suffering from cancer in India.
  • Aditi received the Pride of Rajasthan Award from the government of Rajasthan.
  • She is multitalented and can play the guitar.
  • In 2018, Aditi Hundia was ranked 12th in Times’ Most Desirable Women List.
  • She shares a close friendship with Divya Kasliwal, Femina Miss India Rajasthan finalist 2019, and Ridhima Godha, a fashion designer and stylist.

Conclusion:

Aditi Hundia is a talented Indian model and former beauty pageant titleholder who has made a significant impact in the fashion industry. With her stunning looks, dedication, and supportive nature, she has gained a considerable fan following. Aditi’s relationship with Ishan Kishan has also garnered attention, showcasing their love and support for each other. With her philanthropic efforts and versatile skills, Aditi Hundia continues to inspire others and achieve success in her career.
