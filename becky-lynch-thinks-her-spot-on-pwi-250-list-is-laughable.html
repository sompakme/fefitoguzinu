Becky Lynch Thinks Her Spot On PWI 250 List Is Laughable

The PWI 250 list dropped yesterday, marking the top 250 female wrestlers in the world in 2023. As with any list in the pro wrestling world, it stirred up quite a debate. Becky Lynch saw her place on the list, but it isn’t going to change the fact that she’s still The Man.

As we previously reported, Rhea Ripley made the top of the list. The Nightmare and Bianca Belair were the only two WWE representatives on the top 10. Becky Lynch obviously doesn’t agree with her placement, either.

Becky Lynch saw a post on X about how she made #29 on the top 250 women wrestlers in the world. The Man commented with a couple of laughing emojis, as well as a reminder that her autobiography is in the pipeline.

“29 [LOL emojis 2x]…anyway my book “Becky Lynch – The Man: Not Your Average Girl” is available for pre-order now!”

We will have to see if WWE makes any mention to this list on television. They typically shy away from mentioning PWI lists in their stories, but it’s also a new day in WWE creative under Triple H’s leadership.

Becky Lynch is still the NXT Women’s Champion, and she has a match set against Lyra Valkyria during NXT Halloween Havoc Night 1 on October 24th. Only time will tell how that turns out for The Man, but she will remain a top star in WWE, no matter what a list says.
