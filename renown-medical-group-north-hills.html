Renown Medical Group - North Hills

Overview

NOTE: Primary Care visits are by appointment only. Please check with our Call Center for availability by calling 775-982-5000. Hours are subject to change during holidays, emergencies and other situations. 

Primary care doctors at Renown Medical Group – North Hills see patients of all ages and coordinate medical care including checkups, immunizations, referrals to specialists, lab work, x-ray & imaging and hospital admissions. If you require medical care for a specific condition, your primary care physician can recommend a specialist based on your needs.

We treat:

  • Colds and flu
  • Fevers
  • Earaches
  • Allergic reactions
  • Aches and pains
  • Fractures and sprains
  • Minor cuts and burns
  • Respiratory infections

Key services:

  • Primary care doctors
  • Annual health screenings
  • Referrals to specialists
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
